Lakme Fashion Week

is one of the most prestigious fashion events in India. It is held twice a year in Mumbai and showcases the latest collections from renowned designers as well as emerging talents. The event attracts celebrities, industry insiders, and fashion enthusiasts from all over the country.

India Fashion Week

organized by the Fashion Design Council of India (FDCI), is a prominent fashion event held in Delhi. It features a diverse range of designers, both established and up-and-coming, who showcase their creative collections on the runway. India Fashion Week serves as a platform to promote Indian fashion and craftsmanship.

Bangalore Fashion Week

is a significant fashion event held in the city of Bangalore. It brings together established and emerging designers from across India, offering a platform for them to showcase their collections. The event showcases a mix of traditional and contemporary designs, reflecting the vibrant fashion scene in Bangalore.
